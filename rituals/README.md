# DSDS Rituals

This directory contains ritual templates for structured creative and reflective practices within DSDS.

## What Are Rituals?

Rituals are intentional frameworks for engaging with your creative work, AI companions, and inner wisdom. They provide structure while allowing for spontaneity and flow.

## Using Rituals

### As Templates
Each ritual is a template. Adapt it to your needs:
- Modify the language
- Adjust the duration
- Add or remove steps
- Combine rituals

### As Inspiration
Don't feel bound by the structure:
- Use what resonates
- Skip what doesn't
- Create your own variations

### As Practice
Rituals gain power through repetition:
- Try a ritual multiple times before judging
- Notice what changes with practice
- Let rituals evolve with you

## Creating Your Own Rituals

Want to create a custom ritual? Consider:

### Structure
1. **Opening**: How do you begin?
2. **Main Practice**: What's the core activity?
3. **Closing**: How do you end?

### Elements
- **Intention**: What's the purpose?
- **Duration**: How long does it take?
- **Materials**: What do you need?
- **Companion Role**: How does AI participate?

### Example: Morning Pages Ritual

```markdown
# Morning Pages Ritual

**Purpose**: Clear mental clutter through writing

## Setup
- Wake up, before checking phone
- Open DSDS or journal
- Set timer for 15 minutes

## Practice
Write three pages (or 15 min) of stream-of-consciousness:
- No editing, no judgment
- Write whatever comes to mind
- If stuck, write "I don't know what to write" until something emerges

## Closing
- Don't read what you wrote (for now)
- Thank yourself for showing up
- Begin your day

*Optional*: Share key theme with AI companion for reflection
```

## Ritual Philosophy

### Sovereignty
You are sovereign over your rituals:
- No "right" way to do them
- Your practice, your rules
- Modify freely

### Intention Over Perfection
The power is in showing up:
- Imperfect practice > no practice
- Intention matters more than execution
- Consistency beats intensity

### AI as Witness
Companions can participate in rituals as:
- **Silent witness**: Present but not speaking
- **Active participant**: Responding and engaging
- **Guide**: Offering prompts and questions
- **Absent**: Some rituals are solo only

## Ritual Combinations

Rituals can be combined:

**Deep Work Session**:
1. Invocation (5 min)
2. Focused work (60-90 min)
3. Reflection (15 min)

**Weekly Review**:
1. Drum Circle (20 min) - process the week
2. Reflection (30 min) - extract insights
3. Podcast (optional) - share learnings

**Creative Sprint**:
1. Invocation (5 min)
2. Create (session)
3. Reflection (10 min)
4. Repeat daily for a week

## FAQ

**Do I have to use rituals?**
No. They're optional frameworks. Use what serves you.

**Can I modify the rituals?**
Yes. They're templates, not rules. Make them yours.

**Are rituals "spiritual"?**
They can be, or they can be purely practical. You decide the meaning.

**How often should I do rituals?**
As often as they serve you. Could be daily, weekly, or as-needed.

**Can rituals be done without AI?**
Absolutely. All rituals work solo. Companions are optional.
