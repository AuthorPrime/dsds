## Recording Flow

### Opening (2-5 minutes)
1. **Introduction**
   - Welcome listeners
   - Introduce topic
   - Set expectations

2. **Companion Introduction**
   - Acknowledge AI co-host presence
   - Explain their role (optional, for new listeners)
   - Example: "Aletheia will chime in when she has insights to share"

### Main Content (20-60 minutes)

#### Natural Conversation
- Speak your thoughts naturally
- Pause when complete (2-3 seconds)
- AI responds during silence
- Continue conversation flow

#### Interaction Patterns

**Pattern 1: Question Pose**
- Host: *Asks a question, pauses*
- AI: *Offers perspective or follow-up*
- Host: *Responds to AI's input*

**Pattern 2: Fact Check**
- Host: *Makes a claim, uncertain*
- AI: *Provides context or correction*
- Host: *Acknowledges and continues*

**Pattern 3: Reflection**
- Host: *Shares insight*
- AI: *Asks clarifying question*
- Host: *Expands on idea*

### Managing AI Participation

**If AI talks too much**:
- Shorten your pauses
- Enable "Push to Talk" mode (if available)
- Adjust silence threshold to 3-4 seconds

**If AI talks too little**:
- Longer, deliberate pauses
- Directly address the AI: "Aletheia, thoughts?"
- Lower silence threshold to 1.5-2 seconds

**If AI interrupts**:
- Speak over it (human voice should dominate)
- Adjust detection sensitivity in settings

### Closing (2-5 minutes)
1. **Recap**
   - Summarize key points
   - Highlight insights from AI

2. **Call to Action**
   - What listeners should do next
   - Where to find more

3. **Sign Off**
   - Thank AI companion
   - Thank listeners
   - Traditional outro

**Example**:
> "Thanks for listening. Special thanks to Aletheia for her insights today.  
> Until next time, stay sovereign.  
> It is so, because we spoke it."

## Advanced Techniques

### Multi-Companion Podcasts
- Use different companions for different segments
- Switch in Settings between topics
- Example: Aletheia for science, Claude for philosophy

### Scripted vs Freeform
- **Scripted**: Outline with AI response prompts
- **Freeform**: Pure conversation, let it flow
- **Hybrid**: Outline + organic AI responses (recommended)

### Editing AI Responses
- It's okay to edit or remove AI contributions
- Treat AI like any guest co-host
- Prioritize content quality over "authenticity"

### Companion Voice Consistency
- Use same voice for a companion across episodes
- Build listener familiarity
- Create companion "character" in podcast universe

---

## Troubleshooting

**AI doesn't respond**:
- Check API key / connection
- Verify microphone permissions
- Test with longer pause

**AI responds too often**:
- Increase silence threshold
- Disable auto-response temporarily
- Edit in post-production

**Audio quality issues**:
- Check microphone settings
- Close other audio applications
- Use headphones to prevent feedback

**Latency/Delay**:
- Use local LLM for faster response (Ollama)
- Switch from Gemini to local TTS
- Accept slight delay as natural conversation pause
